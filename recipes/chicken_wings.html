Chicken Wings Recipe

Description

Chicken wings are a popular appetizer or snack, typically deep-fried or baked and coated in a variety of sauces like buffalo, barbecue, or honey mustard. They’re often served with a side of dipping sauce, such as ranch or blue cheese, and are known for their crispy exterior and tender, flavorful meat.

Ingredients:

  • 10-12 chicken wings
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/4 cup buffalo sauce (or your favorite sauce)
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: Fresh parsley for garnish

Steps:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Pat the chicken wings dry with paper towels.
  3. In a large bowl, toss the wings with olive oil, garlic powder, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  4. Place the wings in a single layer on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper or aluminum foil.
  5. Bake for 25-30 minutes, flipping halfway through, until the wings are crispy and golden brown.
  6. Remove from the oven and toss the wings in buffalo sauce (or your chosen sauce).
  7. Serve with dipping sauce and garnish with fresh parsley, if desired. Enjoy!
